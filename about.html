About

The website was created on 1 Apr. 2022. We aim to start a community mainly for Concordia Middle School students, but also anyone, to learn IT (Information Technology) and Cybersecurity by playing CTF (Capture the Flag) games.

What is Cybersecurity?

“Become a hacker, but to protect.”

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting critical systems and sensitive information from digital attacks, also known as information technology (IT) security.
- IBM

Well, it’s not hard to tell that information technology has already become critical in our daily life. From big servers, personal computers to smartphones, or even the air conditioner in your room – you can find technology everywhere. However, with more and more services and new stuff being created, the risk of using these services is also getting higher. That’s the reason to learn cybersecurity. As long as the threat exists, the shield needs to be there, and you can be that shield.

Why create this club?

We find it hard to learn these things in school because when we are learning alone, it's difficult to keep up with the progress and there are few people to discuss the problems.​So here we are! By joining this club, we can solve problems together and have fun at the same time!

Let's do this

Hey, if you would like to help make the world a better place, or just want to gain a better understanding of how things work in the background, consider joining us! We'd love to see what we can achieve in the future!
